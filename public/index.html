Windsor Morris

We are a friendly, energetic and enthusiastic group who enjoy dancing together and taking our dances to a variety of audiences.

We run a ‘taster’ course every Autumn at which you too can learn to Morris dance. The next session will be on Wednesday 25th October from 8pm at the Gardener’s Hall, 213 St. Leonards Rd, Windsor SL4 3DR
