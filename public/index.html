Windsor Morris

We are a friendly, energetic and enthusiastic group who enjoy dancing together and taking our dances to a variety of audiences.

We run a ‘taster’ course every Autumn at which you too can learn to Morris dance.

Keep up to date with what’s happening with Windsor Morris on Facebook and Instagram.
